Why Coding

Stunning Career

Programming is becoming an essential skill. Aside from the tremendous demand for programmers across the world, there are a lot of overlooked benefits of learning how to code

  • Some of the biggest challenges now and in the near future will be solved with a few lines of code.
  • Coding teaches the power of persistence, structured and creative thinking, and plays a vital role for problem solving in our everyday lives

By learning how to code, you can tell computers what to do or how to behave in a much faster way and one of the best ways to start coding is by building website.

  • It is a must for every business to have a website and those who have this skill will play a crucial role in that change.
  • The demand for Web developers is projected to grow 8% from 2019 to 2029 according to a report by Bureau of Labor statistics and this was made evident in the pandemic as businesses were forced to move their business online.
  • Fortunately, this is going to be the new way of running a business and being at the forefront of this new normal is the way to build a profitable career.
